IORG is a Taiwan-based multidisciplinary research group focuses on information operations within the framework of cognitive warfare.

A multidisciplinary, data-driven, rigorous understanding of information operations is needed in order to devise appropriate responses to respond to cognitive warfare, protect Taiwan’s civil liberties, and strengthen Taiwan’s democracy.
